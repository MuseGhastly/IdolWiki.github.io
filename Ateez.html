About the Group

ATEEZ is a dynamic eight-member boy group under KQ Entertainment. Their name, an acronym for "A TEEnager Z," signifies their ambition to captivate audiences from A to Z. Known for their powerful performances, unique world-building concepts, and captivating storytelling, they have built a global fanbase, affectionately known as ATINY.
